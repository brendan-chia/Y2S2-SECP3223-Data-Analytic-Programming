## What I Learned
- I gained practical experience in merging disparate datasets. For example, matching the Malaysia car registrations from Cars2025.csv with the baseline carbon emissions data in FuelConsumption.csv requires standardizing naming conventions to ensure accurate joins without losing valuable data records.
- The project reinforced the importance of the entire data analytics lifecycle. Moving systematically from raw data cleaning and advanced aggregation (group operations) to summary analytics and final visualization taught me how to maintain a clean, readable, and reproducible Jupyter Notebook workflow.
- I learned how to translate data into actionable insights. Structuring the report with well-motivated research questions and tying our final machine learning conclusions back to sustainable solutions taught me how to present data in a meaningful context.
